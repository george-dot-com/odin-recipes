Moka Pot Coffee

The moka pot is an Italian stovetop coffeemaker that produces a brew not unlike an espresso. Steam is forced through the grounds.

Ingredients

  • ground coffee (medium-fine grind)
  • filtered water
  • moka pot

Steps

  1. fill the basket with ground and level the surface. Do not press down on the coffee grounds as this will hamper the steam's pass-through
  2. fill water to the valve
  3. assemble the moka pot and set it on the stove
  4. take off flame when one hears the gurgling of end-brew process
  5. pour and serve
